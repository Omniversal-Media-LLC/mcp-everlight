Absolutely — Premier Protein is a great addition, especially if you're aiming to stay full longer or recover physically (from walking, emotional stress, or poor sleep). It adds around 30g of protein, which balances the smoothie beautifully.

If you’re adding Premier Protein:

  • Reduce or skip the yogurt — it’s not necessary with the shake.
  • Consider using the shake as the liquid base (instead of almond milk), especially if you’ve got vanilla, chocolate, or caramel — all pair surprisingly well with berries and peanut butter.

Here’s how it would look:

Premier Power Smoothie: - 1 banana
- ½ cup frozen pineapple
- ½ cup frozen mixed berries
- Handful of spinach
- ¼ cup oats
- 1 tablespoon peanut butter
- 1 Premier Protein shake (flavor of your choice — vanilla is most versatile)
- Optional: chia seeds, cinnamon, ginger
