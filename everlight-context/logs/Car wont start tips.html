Immediate Options:

  • Pop the hood tonight to allow any residual heat to dissipate faster and reduce under-hood temps overnight. It won’t fix the issue but can help slightly in the morning.
  • Unplug any devices or accessories drawing power from the car — even a small drain can matter when the battery’s weak.
  • If it’s safe and you have jumper cables or a power pack, plan to jump it again in the morning and drive for at least 30 minutes at highway speed if possible.
  • Don’t idle for AC again tonight — I know it’s rough, but that’s what probably drained it in the first place.

To Do Tomorrow:

  • Get the battery tested. AutoZone or O’Reilly’s will usually do it for free. You might be dealing with an old or weak battery that’s no longer holding charge properly.
