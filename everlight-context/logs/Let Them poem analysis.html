This is a powerful and heartfelt poem titled "Let Them" by Cassie Phillips. The central message is one of letting go, radical acceptance, and self-respect. Here's a breakdown of its essence:

  • If people show you—by action or absence—that they don’t value you, let them.
  • Don’t chase, convince, or cling. Let their behavior reveal who they are.
  • In doing so, you protect your own peace, dignity, and emotional space.
  • And if someone truly belongs in your life, they’ll make the effort. Let those people in, too.

It's about knowing your worth, being open but not desperate, and allowing people to either step up or step out—without resistance or resentment.

Letting go isn’t about giving up; it’s about creating space for what’s real.
